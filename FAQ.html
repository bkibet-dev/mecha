Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about our products and services.

Between SSD and HDD which is better?

SSD (Solid State Drive) is generally better than HDD (Hard Disk Drive) because it has faster read/write speeds, is more durable, and consumes less power. However, HDDs are typically more cost-effective for large storage capacities.
